CANCELLATION POLICY: A deposit of the first night's charges will guarantee your reservation for the day of your scheduled arrival. If reservation not cancelled by 2PM PST 1 day before arrival or guest does not show then 1 night will be charged.

EARLY DEPARTURE: If modification is not given to the hotel within 24 hours of departure , an early departure fee of $75 applies to guests checking out prior to the reserved departing date.

ADDITIONAL GUEST: Hotel room rates vary by date and by the number of adults occupying a single room. To accommodate additional guests, please change your reservation to reflect the total guests in a room. Additional fees will apply.

CREDIT CARD POLICY: Debit and credit cards will be authorized at check-in for the amount of your confirmed reservations with an additional $150 held for incidental charges until your check out date. Authorized amounts may take up to 5 to 7 business days after departure to be released and the hotel is not responsible for any fees or charges associated with funds.

PETS: Pets are not allowed at the hotel.

SMOKING POLICY: Smoking is prohibited in all guestrooms and public areas. Smoking is only permitted in designated smoking area and outside the building. A $250 fee will be applicable to guests who violate this policy in order to cover the extensive cost of restoring guest rooms to a smoke-free condition.

DIRECTIONS: From the South (Airport): Head on Highway 101 Northbound, exit Oyster Point Blvd. At Stop light turn left. Proceed into the left turn lane, and at the next two stop lights turn left onto Airport Blvd. Drive Approx. four blocks to the hotel on you right.      From the North (Downtown): Head on Highway 101 Southbound, exit South San Francisco. At stop sign at base of exit make a left onto Airport Blvd. Drive Approx. four blocks to the hotel on you right.
